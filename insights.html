🎯 Strategic Exam Insights
🔥
4 Topics = ~60% of Marks
Descriptive Stats, Bayes, Naïve Bayes, and Normal Distribution appear in every single paper. Master these 4 first — they carry 10–18 marks per paper.
💯
Naïve Bayes = Always 5 Marks
Without exception, Naïve Bayes is a full 5-mark question in every paper. Same algorithm every time — memorize the steps once, guarantee full marks.
📈
Joint Distribution = Biggest Question
Joint distributions appear in 5/6 papers as a 5–6 mark question. Know: marginal, conditional, independence check, E[X], E[Y], E[XY].
📊
Poisson Always Paired with Binomial
In 4 out of 6 papers, Poisson and Binomial appear in the same question. Calculate both, compare results. Know when to approximate: n≥20, p≤0.05.
🧮
Descriptive Stats = Q1 Always
Five-number summary + outlier detection is Q1 in every paper. Easiest guaranteed marks. Sort → Q1/Q2/Q3 → IQR → Lower/Upper bounds → check outliers.
💡
CDF is a New Trend (Jan 2026)
CDF appeared for the first time in Jan 2026 Makeup. Expect it in future papers. Know: find k from CDF conditions, differentiate to get PDF, compute probabilities.
